SKILLS

COMMUNICATION

An engineer needs to be able to communicate well as they may need to speak clearly so other people can understand. They need to be able to understand written and spoken information that is given. Working with other people is also important as they may need to change ideas in relation to someone else's actions. They will need to solve problems by talking about them with other people and being able to understand others' reactions to the possible solutions.

PROBLEM-SOLVING

Engineers will need to be able to have thorough attention to detail as they need to be able to determine if something is wrong or could possibly go wrong. They will also need to be able to combine multiple pieces of information to draw conclusions and discover answers to problems. They will need to recognize the origin of a problem and understand why it might have happened and what types of approaches are needed to solve the problem.

CREATIVITY

They will need to understand patterns and be able to think of original ideas about a topic. Engineers will need to be able to visualize how something may look if it were to be moved around or rearranged. They will also need to quickly understand and compare letters, numbers, objects, pictures, or patterns. They may need to redesign certain tools or materials to meet the requirements needed.
